TRAVELS

Over the past two years my girlfriend and I have traveled to Panama, Mexico City and Cuba. I wanted to visit these countries because I always felt like an outsider growing up. There weren't many Afro Latin kids that I knew and my friends were always confused why my family looked Latin, but why I looked Black. Visiting these places helped me realize what Afro Latin culture was all about. Love, acceptance, warmth and the freedom to drink a beer on any given Tuesday afternoon that I pleased. I wanted to share these pictures to show the strength and beauty of the places that helped me embrace who I am.
