ABOUT ME

My name is Jeremi Dudu and currently I work as a UX Designer for Rotten Tomatoes. Our site hasn't been redesigned in the past 20 years, but we're working on it!

I believe that successful products should feel as inclusive as possible which is something I naturally keep in mind when figuring out design solutions.

Although I may not have had formal training as a designer, in my past lives I've worked as a Barber, Social Media Manager, DJ and Teacher. Through each occupation I've formed a foundation of being able to have the vision to form a strategy while having the confidence to execute, iterate and build something my team can feel proud of. One of my greatest strengths and true passions as a designer is helping the people around me to personally develop. Whether its through fostering soft skills or learning a new technology tool.

As product designers, we constantly think about how we can get people coming back. But as a Barber and DJ, I learned that consistently providing top notch experiences would allow your customers to do the marketing for you while you continue to iterate day in and day out.

Fun Facts:
- My mother was from Belize and my father is from Nigeria
- I skateboarded for 12 years; will always be a skateboarder at heart
- I've had the chance to live in Lower East Side, New York and Sydney, Australia
