By now you've learned that I'm not your typical UI/UX Designer...

My original plan was to work in sales with a YouTube network and change our culture through entertainment programming. But my desire to help my previous teams as much as I can, sparked my curiousity and helped me find my true passions. After attending General Assembly, I worked a contract job as a UX Designer at a small agency in Downtown LA where I was able to validate that I really enjoyed the UX field. Every day involved selling ideas or solving problems through design. Suprisingly my broad background prepared me to work as a web designer.

Working as a barber for 10 years translated in to the same thought process by having an eye for small details and understanding my clients' needs. During college I DJ'd parties that helped me understand the importance of great timing. The same applies in web animations to delight users. Working in social media helped me understand the importance of testing. We don't need to have all the answers by the time we launch, but we should learn and improve what we do day after day.

Working at M-GO/Fandango NOW has been a complete blessing since, I've grown my skill set and I enjoy my role on our team. I've been able to grow my visual design skills, my technical knowledge and learn about different fields of artistic expression. I'm passionately curious which has allowed me to continuously develop my craft and eventually I'd like to be in a position where I can help others grow professionally.
