My Story

If you can't already tell, I love creating digital experiences. I've worked in several digital marketing roles over the past two years and I learned how to code because I'm creative, but I wanted to challenge my self to have a technical skill set as well. Recently I finished up an awesome coding bootcamp at General Assembly.

Before GA I was a Coach and Mentor at an organization called STOKED where I taught life skills through action sports. I also helped launch an entertainment website for AwesomenessTV. Currently I work as a contracted UX/UI Designer at an agency called Drawing From Memory in Downtown LA. I love bringing visions to fruition and I am unafraid to push my boundaries.

I constantly strive for self improvement and when I want to learn something, I prefer to dive in and start tinkering. When not coding or designing, I skateboard, surf, drop buckets on Kobe Bryant wannabes at the local park or DJ parties! Having passion for what I do has allowed me to develop a diverse skill set, but also be confident along the path I continue to take.
