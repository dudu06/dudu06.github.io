My Story

After graduating from USC in 2012, my original plan was to work in sales for a YouTube network. That didn't happen...

I am a digital marketer turned front end developer. I've worked in several digital marketing roles over the past two years. The reason I wanted to learn how to code is because I'm naturally creative, but I wanted to match my creativity with technical capabilities. Recently I finished up an awesome coding bootcamp at General Assembly.

Before GA I was a Coach and Mentor at an organization called STOKED where I taught life skills through action sports such as surfing and skateboarding. I also helped launch a site for AwesomenessTV. In the future I'd like to pursue a position as a UI or UX Designer. I believe that user friendly design is just as important as functionality. But the main reason I'd like to pursue the field I want to get in to is because I want to help businesses connect with users in ways that feel organic. I think that businesses thrive when users feel connected to products that make them feel good.

When not coding or designing, I skateboard, surf, drop buckets on Kobe Bryant wannabes at the local park or DJ parties! I truly love the work that I do which has allowed me to develop a wide skill set along the path I continue to take.
