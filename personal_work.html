PERSONAL DESIGN WORK

I decided to start a creative project called Boo The Savior in the summer of 2017. During this period I tried things out of my comfort zone like creating custom furniture and delving into cooking Thai cuisine. Boo the Savior was one of those uncomfortable ideas I toyed with that became a clothing brand. But I wanted it to become an experience that would come alive through art, history, music and cultural events. The concept was inspired by the transient effects of modernity seeking primitive clarity through spirituality. Currently I'm working on an exhibition...stay tuned to see how that goes!
